# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to maintaining a productive, respectful, and professional environment for all contributors.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Communicate with clarity and respect
- Accept constructive feedback professionally
- Focus on technical merit

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Sharing confidential project information outside authorized channels
- Deliberate disruption of collaborative work

## Enforcement

Violations may result in removal from the organization.
Report issues to the organization owner via GitHub.
